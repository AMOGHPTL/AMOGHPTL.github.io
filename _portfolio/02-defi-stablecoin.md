An overcollateralized, decentralized stablecoin pegged to $1.00. Minting is algorithmic — the stablecoin can only be minted against sufficient crypto collateral — and the peg is anchored with Chainlink price feeds.

- Exogenous crypto collateral: wETH and wBTC.
- Chainlink price feeds for pricing collateral.
- Built and tested with Foundry.
