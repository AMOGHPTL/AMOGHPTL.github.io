**Syphon** is a lending and borrowing protocol where users supply collateral and borrow against it, while liquidity providers earn yield by supplying the markets of their choice. It is a standalone protocol built from scratch — not a fork of Aave or Morpho.

- Supply collateral and borrow against it at high LTV.
- Provide liquidity to a chosen market and earn yield.
- Liquidate unhealthy positions for a bonus.
- Isolated markets per token pair to contain risk.
- Adaptive Curve interest-rate model that adjusts borrow rates by utilization.
