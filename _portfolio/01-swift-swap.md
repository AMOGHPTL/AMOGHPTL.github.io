**Swift-Swap** is a decentralized exchange for ERC-20 tokens, in the spirit of Uniswap and SushiSwap. It uses a constant-product automated market maker (AMM), so traders can swap tokens directly on-chain with no centralized intermediary.

- Swap any supported ERC-20 token pair on-chain.
- Provide liquidity to pools and earn yield in the form of liquidity tokens.
- Create custom pools by choosing a fee and a token pair.
