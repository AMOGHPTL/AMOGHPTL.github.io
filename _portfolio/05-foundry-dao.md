An on-chain governance system where a contract is controlled by a DAO. Every transaction the DAO wants to execute must be proposed and voted on, using ERC-20 tokens for voting power, and is then carried out through a timelock.

- Proposal, voting, and execution flow.
- ERC-20 governance token for voting power.
- Timelock-controlled execution of passed proposals.
